## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Shortcut Genius turns plain-English ideas into real iOS Shortcuts.

Type or speak something like "remind me to call Mom when I leave work" or "play sleep sounds when I plug in to charge" — the app builds you a working Shortcut you can install with one tap.

WHAT YOU GET

• 100+ ready-made shortcuts across Driving, Focus, Family, Work, Health, Smart Home, Travel, Productivity, Creative, and Wellness.
• A natural-language creator. Type or speak any idea and get a custom Shortcut, ready to install.
• A personal library of every Shortcut you've made or saved, with edit and re-generate options.
• Home-screen widgets so your favourite shortcuts are always one tap away.
• Native App Intents — your shortcuts appear in the Shortcuts app, in Spotlight, and in Siri.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• Everything is stored locally on your device. There is no account, no cloud sync, no telemetry.
• Bring your own AI key (OpenAI or Grok) only when you want cloud generation. Keys live in your iPhone's Keychain — we never see them.
• On-device fallback matches your idea against our 100+ built-ins so the app is genuinely useful with zero cloud calls.

NON-GREEDY PRICING

• Free forever: 100+ examples + 10 AI generations per month.
• Premium $19.99/year (with a 7-day free trial): unlimited AI generations, advanced voice input, full export and history.
• Lifetime $49 one-time: same as Premium, paid once.
• Optional $4.99 tip ("Buy the dev a coffee") if you'd like to say thanks.

The paywall has a "Free forever is fine!" button on every screen. We mean it.

REQUIRES iOS 18 OR LATER.

— Made by an indie dev who actually uses this thing every day. Feedback welcome.
```
